Hello, I'm Gaganpreet Kaur, a dynamic professional with a unique journey from academia to data science, infusing a passion for extracting insights from data and transforming them into impactful business solutions.

In my 4-year role as an Assistant Professor at the Ramgarhia Institute of Technology, I not only effectively communicated complex concepts in Engineering Physics but also developed a keen interest in data management and analysis tools, particularly Microsoft Excel. This sparked my transition into the world of data science during a postgraduate academic project, leading to a transformative experience in BrainStation's Data Science Diploma Program.

Amidst the challenges of the pandemic, I reinvented my professional path, unveiling a newfound passion for coding and data science. Engaging in the EY & BrainStation Remote 3D Hackathon, I collaborated on "Safe Eats," a digital solution for small-medium businesses during the COVID-19 era. This immersive program equipped me with proficiency in SQL, Python, Tableau, and various machine and deep learning techniques.

My journey extends into healthcare, where I managed the comprehensive lifecycle of patient data within the HCL Database. Collaborating seamlessly with cross-functional teams, I addressed and resolved issues, optimizing data management processes. Additionally, I administered financial reports using QuickBooks and utilized Microsoft Excel for inventory management, showcasing my versatility in data management tools.

In retail, I led a team, educated associates, and established a new overnight team at Walmart. Utilizing advanced Microsoft Excel data tools, including VBA, Pivot Tables, and Excel Formulas, I identified sales patterns, monitored inventory, and analyzed product offerings. Collaboration with management resulted in an impressive 70% increase in retention rates.

What sets me apart is not only my technical prowess but also my collaborative and leadership skills. Whether in academia, retail, or healthcare, I bring a holistic approach to problem-solving. As a physicist turned data analyst, I've leveraged analytical skills to solve complex problems in diverse sectors.

I am excited about the prospect of applying my diverse skill set to new challenges and contributing to the success of your organization. My commitment to delivering impactful business solutions, coupled with a collaborative approach, positions me as a valuable asset for any role or company.
